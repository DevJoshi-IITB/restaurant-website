Menu

Burger

A succulent, flame-grilled beef patty topped with aged cheddar cheese, caramelized onions, fresh arugula, and juicy heirloom tomatoes. Finished with a dollop of house-made aioli and served on a toasted brioche bun. Accompanied by a side of crispy sweet potato fries.

Pizza

A classic favorite featuring a hand-tossed crust, zesty tomato sauce, and generous layers of mozzarella cheese. Topped with spicy pepperoni slices and baked in our stone oven for a crispy, flavorful finish.

Pasta

Al dente spaghetti tossed in a creamy sauce made with eggs, Parmesan cheese, and crispy pancetta. Finished with a sprinkle of freshly ground black pepper and a garnish of parsley for an authentic Italian experience.

About Us

Welcome to Gusteau's! We are passionate about bringing people together through great food and a warm, inviting atmosphere. Since our founding in 2001, we have been dedicated to using the freshest, locally sourced ingredients to create delicious dishes that blend traditional and contemporary flavors. From our succulent burgers to our authentic Italian pizzas and pastas, every item on our menu is crafted with care and love. Our friendly staff is committed to providing you with an exceptional dining experience, making Gusteau's the perfect place for any occasion. Join us and create lasting memories over a meal that's as delightful as it is memorable.
